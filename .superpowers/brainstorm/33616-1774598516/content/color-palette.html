Color Direction

Your current palette is soft mint/warm. Duolingo is bold and saturated. Which direction?

A. Pure Duolingo

Adopt Duolingo's exact palette: Feather Green (#58CC02), Macaw Blue, Cardinal Red, Bee Yellow, Beetle Purple. White backgrounds, grey neutrals. Bold and saturated.

B. Duolingo-ified DevPet

Keep your mint green (#34D399) as primary but pump up the saturation. Add bold secondary colors inspired by Duolingo. Apply their 3D button style and bold typography to your existing palette.

C. Fresh Hybrid

New palette from scratch — brighter, bolder greens with neutral grey backgrounds (not warm tinted). Duolingo's design patterns with completely fresh colors that feel unique to DevPet.
